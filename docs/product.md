# Product

## Positioning

Grand Ops is the AI operations assistant for teams that live in chat. It turns
messages into tracked, approved, auditable work.

## First Customer

The first customer should be a small service business or agency with high chat
volume and low process maturity:

- marketing agencies
- repair shops
- consulting teams
- solo founders with contractors
- coaches or course operators
- small support teams

These users already coordinate in Slack, WhatsApp, Telegram, Discord, and email.
They do not want another heavy project management system before they feel the
pain of missing work.

## Core Promise

Grand catches work where it appears, asks before doing risky things, executes
safe tasks, and leaves receipts.

## MVP Jobs

- Capture a customer or teammate request from chat.
- Convert it into a task with owner, status, source, and risk level.
- Ask for human approval before external or destructive actions.
- Run approved safe work.
- Produce a daily digest and task reports.

## Commands

```text
grand status
grand approve <task-id>
grand reject <task-id>
grand done <task-id>
```

The command style should stay short enough for messaging channels.

## What Not To Build First

- A broad consumer personal assistant.
- A full CRM.
- A full ticketing platform.
- Autonomous payments or account changes without approval trails.
- Complex custom workflow builders.

The first version should win by being useful in messy chat, not by replacing
every business system.
